# PM Copilot

A terminal-based AI assistant for software teams that brings the feel of Claude Code to project management.

## What is this?

PM Copilot is an agentic CLI tool that acts as an intelligent layer on top of your existing project management workflow. Rather than switching between your terminal, your issue tracker, and your codebase, you interact with a single conversational agent that understands all three.

## What can it do?

- **Ticket creation & triage** — describe a problem, a feature, or a bug in plain language and the agent will create well-structured tickets, assign them, and slot them into the right place in your backlog.
- **Sprint planning** — get suggestions for sprint scope based on team capacity, historical velocity, and ticket complexity.
- **Codebase-aware suggestions** — the agent understands your codebase, so its recommendations are grounded in how your system actually works rather than generic advice.
- **Standup & status summaries** — generate standup updates, surface blockers, and summarize team activity without digging through ticket history manually.

## Philosophy

PM Copilot is designed to stay out of your way. It works from your terminal, integrates with the tools you already use, and always shows you what it's about to do before it does it. The goal isn't to replace your judgement — it's to handle the mechanical overhead so you can focus on the work that matters.
